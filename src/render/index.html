Welcome to LUCI

The Laboratory for Ubiquitous Computing and Interaction is a research group at the University of California, Irvine that brings together faculty and students in the School of Information and Computer Science who share research interests in the design and use of ubiquitous computing platforms, infrastructures, technologies, and applications.
